Expand description

Compares each slot of lhs against each slot of rhs.

Error

Errors iff:

  • lhs.data_type() != rhs.data_type() or
  • lhs.len() != rhs.len() or
  • the datatype is not supported (use can_compare to tell whether it is supported)
